Neural signatures of semantic prediction in bilingual language comprehension

Hou, F., Smith, A., Johnson, B., & Martinez, C.

Journal of Cognitive Neuroscience, 36(3), 445-462

This fMRI and ERP study investigated how bilingual speakers predict upcoming words during sentence comprehension. Results revealed distinct neural networks for prediction in L1 vs L2, with individual differences linked to proficiency and age of acquisition.
